Fresh Lumpia

What Is A Fresh Lumpia

Fresh Lumpia is a unique version of spring roll from the Philippines. It is a combination of meat and vegetable and fresh lettuce topped with sweeten sauce and penut, giving it a beautiful blend of sweet and salt flavor which makes it an instant favourite in any occasion

Ingredients

Filling
  • 2 Tablespoon vegetable Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 piece onion, chopped
  • 1 piece carrot, peeled and cut into strips
  • 250 grams mung bean sprout
  • 1 head small cabbage, shredded
  • 250 grams tofu, cooked and cut into cubs
  • 200 grams shrimps, boiled and peeled
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

Lumpia Wrapper
  • 1 1/2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/4 water
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon vegatble oil

Lumpia Sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 1/4 brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 head of garlic, chopped

Procedure

  1. To make the lumpia filling, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable in a pan and saut√© garlic and onion.
  2. Add carrots, Mung Beans Sprout, Cabbage and tofu. Cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Season with soy sauce.
  4. To make the batter for the lumpia wrapper, combine eggs, water, all-purpose flour, salt and vegetable oil.
  5. Heat a small non-stick pan and ladle out 1/3 cup measure of the lumpia wrapper.
  6. Cook one side and then slide out of the pan. Set aside and continue with the rest.
  7. To make the sauce, combine water, brown sugar, cornstarch, soy sauce and garlic in a saucepan, Simmer them until the mixture thickens. set aside.
  8. Measure about 2 tablespoons of vegetable filling per wrapper and top with shrimp. Roll and serve with lumpia sauce.
